Maria is a spiritual oil paint artist whose work explores the intersection of devotion, nature, and the human form. Through rich color palettes and expressive brushwork, her paintings invite contemplation and connection with the sacred.

Working primarily in oil on canvas, her subjects range from Buddhist and Hindu deities to dreamlike figurative scenes, luminous landscapes, and meditative abstractions. Each piece is an offering — a visual prayer rendered in pigment and light.
